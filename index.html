What is Anxiety and What Causes It?

Anxiety is a normal experience for everyone. It helps us stay safe, motivates us to take action, and helps us stay alert. However, it can become problematic when it interferes with our daily lives.

Causes of Anxiety:

  • Lack of preparation
  • Negative thought patterns
  • Physical symptoms like an increased heart rate and shallow breathing
  • Not taking care of your mental and physical health (sleep, diet, exercise)

Managing Anxiety:

  • Self-care (sleep, diet, exercise)
  • Preparation and positive self-talk
  • Breathing exercises and relaxation techniques
